Scrambled Eggs



Description

Scrambled eggs are a quick and easy breakfast made by whisking eggs and gently cooking them until soft and fluffy. Theyâ€™re delicious on their own or served with toast, and can be seasoned with salt, pepper, or herbs for extra flavor. Perfect for a simple morning meal!

ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp butter
  • salt and pepper

steps

  1. Beat the eggs in a bowl
  2. Melt butter in a pan on low heat
  3. pour in the eggs and stir slowly until it become fluffy
  4. add salt and pepper to taste
