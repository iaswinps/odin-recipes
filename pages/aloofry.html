Aloo Fry



Description

Aloo Fry is a simple and delicious dish made from crispy fried potatoes seasoned with turmeric, chili, and salt. It makes a perfect side dish or snack and can be enjoyed hot for maximum taste.

ingredients

  • 2 medium potatoes
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp chili powder (optional)
  • salt to taste
  • 2 tsp oil

steps

  1. Peel and thinly slice the potatoes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan on medium heat.
  3. Add potatoes, turmeric, chili powder, and salt.
  4. Fry while stirring occasionally until golden and crispy
